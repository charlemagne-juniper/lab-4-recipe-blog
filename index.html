About

Welcome! I'm Charley (he/him). As a Twin Cities-based designer with a focus on ergonomic design, I am passionate about creating products that prioritize both form and function. With an education in Product Design from Minneapolis College of Art and Design, I bring a strong foundation in design principles and techniques, as well as extensive experience collaborating with diverse teams and exhibiting my work at events such as the MCAD Sculpture Exhibition and Saint Louis Park Art Fair. Additionally, I am recently certified in HTML5 and CSS demonstrating my commitment to staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends.
